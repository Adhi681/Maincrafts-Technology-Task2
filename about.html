About TravelBuddy

Built for explorers. Designed for adventure.

Our Story

TravelBuddy was built for explorers who love the outdoors. Whether you're planning a multi-day trek through the mountains, a long-distance bike tour, or a relaxed weekend getaway, our smart itinerary builder helps you map routes, find stays, and discover trails — all in one place.

We believe great trips start with great planning. That's why our platform combines real traveler reviews, offline maps, and budget tracking so you can focus on the journey, not the logistics.
